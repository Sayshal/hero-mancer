A complete, ground-up rewrite.

**Breaking**
- Requires Foundry VTT v14+.
- Most 2.x settings aren't carried forward; removed keys are purged and a few migrated on first load.
- Localization keys restructured; non-English translations dropped pending retranslation.

**New**
- Rebuilt frameless wizard: drag/resize, per-user position, live progress bars and missing-field badges, and a one-click Randomize.
- Reworked Identity, Abilities, HP, Advancements, and Equipment tabs with searchable pickers and live validation.
- Interactive Advancements (ability, proficiency, and item/feature choices) with a Granted summary; auto-grants applied at creation.
- Equipment tab with a shop, gold budget, "take wealth instead" toggles, and GM bonus-gold formulas.
- Level-Up mode: multiclassing, prerequisite checks, and a live preview of HP, features, and subclasses.
- Optional GM approval queue: players submit, GMs review/approve/reject in-wizard.
- Compare and pin, a feat browser, and a live sidebar HUD.
- Integrations: Tokenizer 2, Spell Book, Calendaria, and Dice So Nice.

**Removed**
- The Enable toggle, Mandatory Fields configurator, character-detail CSV pools, roll-table biography, and CPR integration.

Full changelog: https://github.com/Sayshal/hero-mancer/releases
