**New Features**
- Added a GM setting to disable custom backgrounds, removing the Custom Background option from the background picker.
- Added a size picker on the Advancements tab for species that offer more than one size; single-size species apply automatically.

**Bug Fixes**
- Fixed item-choice advancement picks, such as a Druid's Primal Order, being silently dropped on dnd5e 5.x.

**Changes**
- Updated the Brazilian Portuguese (pt-BR) translation.
