## Breaking Changes

- Hero Mancer now requires the 3DS:ATLAS module, which centralizes logging, update notices, localization, and theming. The standalone release-message popup and per-module log-level setting have been removed.

## New Features

- During level-up, item-choice advancements that allow replacement now offer an optional picker to swap out one item chosen at an earlier level.
- Level-up now surfaces advancements from already-owned items (such as general feats) under a new "Features" group.
- The custom background builder now includes a language picker, with a new setting controlling how many languages a custom background grants.
- The troubleshooter report can now optionally bundle your open wizard's current build (selected species, background, class/subclass, equipment, and granted items).

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed custom backgrounds losing advancements: the ability score improvement and trait grants now all persist instead of collapsing onto a single advancement.
- Fixed the open-feat picker hiding custom or homebrew feats that have no prerequisite level. Feats without a level requirement now appear, while level-gated feats above the cap stay hidden.
- HP and wealth rolls that are published to chat now animate only once through Dice So Nice, instead of playing a duplicate local animation.
- The custom background builder footer is now pinned and no longer clips its contents.

## Changes

- Custom backgrounds now show their own generated description in the preview panel instead of a same-named compendium journal page.
- Removed the bundled localization files; translations now come from the required 3DS:ATLAS module.
- Subclass previews now show the subclass description above the advancement table, followed by its features.
