**New Features**
- Added a Custom Background entry to the background picker that opens a builder for abilities, skills, tools, and an origin feat, then stores the generated background in a world pack.
- Added GM settings for the custom background limits: ability choices, points, cap, skill/tool/feat counts, and starting gold.

**Bug Fixes**
- An out-of-range manually entered ability score no longer blocks Approve in GM review.
- Fixed character data loss in the GM review flow: player color-picker values now persist, equipment grants restore from the draft, and single checkboxes restore correctly.
- Fixed the expertise tile so stale picks auto-clear when a skill is no longer proficient, and current picks are listed in the picker.
- Made the HP method caret clickable so clicking anywhere on the method chip opens the method select.

**Changes**
- Fixed Ability Score Improvements now auto-apply, and ScaleValue and ModifyItem advancements auto-apply.
- Prewarmed identity and equipment indexes for faster cold GM review opens, with a loading splash on cold open and a Randomization complete toast after Randomize All.
- Reopening Tokenize now reloads saved Tokenizer 2 layers for non-destructive re-edit.
- Reworded the equipment wealth note to say remaining gold will be added to sheet.
- The skip-spell-handoff checkbox in Finalize now shows only for spellcasters when the Spell Book module is active.
- Updated the Brazilian Portuguese (pt-BR) translation.
