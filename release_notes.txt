**New Features**
- Added a Manual Entry ability score method that lets players type scores directly, clamped to the configured minimum and maximum.

**Bug Fixes**
- Fixed a crash when selecting a class whose starting equipment offers an open category choice, such as any spellcasting focus.
- Fixed the equipment purchase filter and sort menus clipping behind the sidebar on narrow windows.

**Changes**
- Locked abilities in the ability score improvement dialog are now greyed out with a tooltip, and abilities open for assignment are highlighted.
- Updated the Brazilian Portuguese (pt-BR) translation.
